Therapy space

Emotional Release

For when you're carrying sadness, anger, or numbness. This is your space to let it out.

😔

Sadness

Sadness is not weakness. It is the soul's way of processing loss, disappointment, or unmet needs.

→

Write a letter to yourself with no filter — say everything you've been holding

→

Allow yourself one good cry — it releases stored emotional pain physically

→

Put your hand on your heart and breathe slowly for 2 minutes

→

Listen to music that matches your mood first — then gradually shift to something uplifting

→

Tell someone how you're really feeling — not "I'm fine"

🎵 Music therapy for sadness
😤

Anger

Anger always has something underneath it — usually hurt, fear, or a boundary that was crossed. Let's find it.

→

Write everything you're angry about without stopping — then tear the paper

→

Ask yourself: what boundary was crossed? What did I need that I didn't get?

→

Move your body — run, walk fast, do jumping jacks — let the anger move through you

→

Breathe in for 4 counts, hold 4, out for 6 — repeat 5 times

→

Write a letter to whoever hurt you — you don't have to send it

🎵 Music therapy for anger
😶

Numbness

Numbness is what happens when you've felt too much for too long. It's protection. But you can gently come back.

→

Hold something cold or warm and focus entirely on the sensation — reconnect with your body

→

Write: "I feel nothing because..." and see what comes out

→

Put on a song that once meant something to you and sit with it

→

Talk to someone — anyone — even about something small

→

Allow yourself to want something today — what would you choose if anything were possible?

🎵 Music therapy for numbness
