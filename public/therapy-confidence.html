Confidence & Self-Worth

You were not born doubting yourself. That was learned. And anything learned can be unlearned.

💪

Building Confidence

Confidence is not something you feel before you act. It's what grows when you act despite fear.

→

Write 10 things you have done that you didn't think you could — they don't have to be big

→

Do one small scary thing today — confidence is built action by action, not thought by thought

→

Stop waiting until you feel ready — readiness comes after, not before

→

Notice when you speak badly about yourself and replace it with what you'd say to a friend

🌱

Protecting Your Self-Worth

Other people's opinions are about them — their fears, their insecurities, their limited view of you. They are not facts.

→

When someone judges you, ask: "Do I respect this person's opinion enough to let it define me?"

→

Write: "My worth is not determined by..." and list everything you've been measuring it with

→

Manipulation only works when you need something from the manipulator — identify what that is

→

Set one boundary this week — and keep it even when it feels uncomfortable

🫂

Allow Yourself to Feel

Suppressing emotions doesn't make them disappear. It makes them louder in ways you don't expect — anxiety, numbness, sudden outbursts.

→

Name the emotion without judging it: "I am feeling jealous" not "I shouldn't feel jealous"

→

Give the feeling 90 seconds — research shows emotions peak and begin to fade within 90 seconds if you don't feed them with thought

→

Write: "It's okay that I feel this because..." — give yourself permission

→

Weird emotions are not signs of being broken — they are signs of being human

💙 Deep patterns need deeper work

If low self-worth has been your reality for a long time, a therapist can help you trace it to its roots and genuinely change it.
